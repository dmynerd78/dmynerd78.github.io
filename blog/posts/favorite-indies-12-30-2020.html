Favorite Indies Over the Decade

December 30, 2020

With the year coming to a close, I figure I would share some of my favorite games in the past decade. Many of these games are singleplayer only since that's normally what I play - especially on my Switch. Below are the banners of the games. Clicking on an image will show more info about them below the group of images. I have also included links to their US digital store pages so you can learn more about them if they interest you.

I've split up my favorites into two categories so each has their own dedicated description area. This is by no means an all-encompassing list; it is simply a handful of games that I keep coming back to even after I finish them (I also haven't played every indie game released in the past decade so again not all-encompassing). If you have others that you think should be added, feel free to share them with your friends!
