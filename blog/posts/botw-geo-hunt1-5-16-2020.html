Breath of the Wild Geo Hunt

May 16, 2020

Hey there! Welcome to my first article! I've actually had this idea in the back of my head since 2017 (crazy to think it's been out for that long) but I never actually finished it. I had the idea while I was replaying through the game again and I thought it'd be super fun. So I've rounded up a handful from my screenshots folder to try out!

Geo Hunt?

I'm not sure if "Geo Hunt" is the proper term, but the concept itself is straight forward. Basically I've taken screenshots of random locations all around Hyrule. Your job is to find where exactly these pictures were taken. Try using context clues and hints to figure out where exactly it is! If you find them all of them you get free cookies!*

If you need to see the image in higher detail, just click the image itself for the full resolution picture. If you're stumped with an image, you can click the black hint box beneath an image for a bit more info. Happy hunting!

Hope you have fun doing this little hunt. Let me know how many you find!

If you really like it, let me know — I may soon have enough for a second geo hunt. And who knows, I may revisit this concept when BotW's sequel comes out.
